Aimee Smith
Director - 7th year with the program

Aimee began her volleyball career as a freshman at St. Charles East High School in Illinois. She played four years for her high school team as a starting middle hitter. Throughout her high school career she played club volleyball with POV and Sports Performance Volleyball Club. During this time she received multiple awards on her way to receiving a college scholarship to Northern Michigan University. As a freshman at Northern Michigan University in 2001 she worked her way to a starting position as a middle hitter. During her tenure at Northern Michigan 2001-2005 she earned the honors of GLIAC North Division Player of the week on numerous occasions in 2003, All-GLIAC First Team Selection, Daktronics All Great Lakes Region Team, AVCA All Great Lakes Region Team in 2004, AVCA Third Team All- American, GLIAC All- Conference First Team Selection, NCAA II Great Lakes Regional All- Tournament Team, AVCA All Great Lakes Region Team, and AVCA Second Team All- American in 2004. Aimee was offered a position to play on the USA Athletes International Volleyball Team in Germany and Holland during her senior year. Upon completing her volleyball career at Northern Michigan University she accepted a position as the head volleyball Coach at Marquette Senior High School. She coached her JV and Varsity teams to the high school volleyball playoffs. Rising through the ranks of high school coaching, Aimee decided to make her mark in another arena. She moved to the Dallas area where she signed on as a coach with Dallas Force. Under the tutelage of Forrest Medcalf she continued to hone her skills in the volleyball arena. Aimee is a highly ethical coach, an excellent communicator, well organized in managing the volleyball business and is an outstanding facilitator when communicating with the players and their parents. She is an enthusiastic coach who motivates her players to be positive, aggressive, and have a hunger for succeeding in the game of volleyball. Her hard work and due diligence has paid off. On July 2, 2012, Aimee DeWitte was named Director of Dallas Force Volleyball Club. Aimee welcomes the parents and players to join her in another outstanding season of Club Volleyball with Dallas Force. She brings an intense coaching mentality to Force that will elevate the level of play and quality of the game. We are looking forward to another successful season!!

Dan Turner
10th year with the program

Dan has been coaching young ladies for fifteen years. He has been with the Dallas Force Volleyball Club for the last nine seasons, working in the Texoma area with 13’s, 14’s, 15’s and 16’s.
