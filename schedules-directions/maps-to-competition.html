Maps to Competition

ACE - Arlington Courts

3120 E Pioneer Pk Arlington, TX 76010

From Carrollton, take PGB Turnpike southwest to the south side of DFW Airport at 183. Get on 183 westbound for a short time and get on 360 going south past Six Flags. Go to the Pioneer Parkway exit and go left (east). The facility will be on the right. It is roughly 15 minutes from ASC.

ASC - Advantage Sports Center

4302 Buckingham Rd Ft Worth, TX 76115

Now that GBT is open all the way to the south end of DFW Airport, that can be taken all the way to it's end and then get on 183 heading towards Ft Worth. Go past the Airport entrance and take the first exit (Amon Carter). Turn left on Amon Carter and go under 183 to FAA Boulevard. Turn left on FAA and right on Buckingham. It is on the left. There is a sign on the side of the building, and you'll see a lot of cars starting to converge on the site.
