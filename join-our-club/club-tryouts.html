Club Tryouts

Please check back prior to going to the tryout. Please plan to arrive 30-minutes early to register (no earlier than 30-minutes). If parents are not planning to attend the entire tryout, they should plan to return to the venue no later than 45-minutes before the tryout is scheduled to be complete. Tryout Fee is $50. You will need to complete some paperwork prior to the tryout and register to be a member of USA Volleyball.
